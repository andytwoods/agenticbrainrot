Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AgenticBrainrot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you participate in our research study.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including when you register for an account, complete surveys, and participate in coding challenges.

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication details via Google or GitHub.
  • Profile Information: Demographic data (age, gender, location) and coding background.
  • Research Data: Coding challenge solutions, timing data, and self-report survey responses.
  • Optional Data: Think-aloud transcripts and reminder email preferences (if consented).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for research purposes, specifically to study the effects of AI-assisted coding on coding skills over time. Aggregate, anonymized data may be published or shared with the research community.

3. Data Retention

Personal identifiers are kept for the duration of the study. Anonymized research data is retained indefinitely for scientific reproducibility.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to withdraw from the study at any time and request the deletion of your personal data.
